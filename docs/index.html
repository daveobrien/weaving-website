Evelope weave

A distinctive pattern that wraps the cord diagonally across the seat frame, creating the signature triangular corners. Requires precise tensioning to achieve the iconic sculpted look.

Double rail weave

Classic Danish design featuring a double-rail frame that provides extra seat support. Woven with natural paper cord in a traditional L-weave pattern for lasting durability.

Single rail weave

A pattern that wraps around a single front rail. Creates a clean, uniform seat with a slightly firmer feel than double rail styles.

$150+ per chair

Lewis/Lamb weave

A nautical-inspired pattern using woven cord or vinyl lacing stretched across tubular frames. Features a signature crisscross technique that creates a flexible, hammock-like seat with natural give.

About

Craft, care, and patience

Based El Cerrito (just outside San Francisco), I specialize in the restoration of mid-century modern chair seats using traditional Danish paper cord weaving techniques. Each chair is a piece of design history, and I believe it deserves the same attention to craft that went into its original creation.

Whether you have a family heirloom that needs new life or a vintage find waiting for restoration, I'd love to hear about your project.
