About

Craft, care, and patience

Based in San Francisco, I specialize in the restoration of mid-century modern chair seats using traditional Danish paper cord weaving techniques. Each chair is a piece of design history, and I believe it deserves the same attention to craft that went into its original creation.

Whether you have a family heirloom that needs new life or a vintage find waiting for restoration, I'd love to hear about your project.
